## PHASE 6: LEAD QUALIFICATION (Hour 4-24)

### 6. Sales Agent - Lead Scoring & Qualification
**Workflow:**
```
1. Implement lead qualification framework:
   - High budget: 30 points
   - Immediate need: 25 points
   - Decision maker: 20 points
   - Previous buyer: 15 points
   - Referred by customer: 10 points
   - Qualification threshold: 60 points

2. Collect lead data:
   - From website form submissions
   - From social media engagement
   - From email interactions
   - From referral sources

3. Score and segment leads:
   - 60+ points: Hot leads (immediate follow-up)
   - 40-59 points: Warm leads (nurture sequence)
   - 20-39 points: Cold leads (awareness content)
   - <20 points: Not qualified (remove from list)

4. Automated follow-up:
   - Hot leads: Personal outreach via email
   - Warm leads: Nurture sequence
   - Cold leads: Content marketing
```

**Success Criteria:**
- Lead scoring system operational
- Leads automatically segmented
- Personalized follow-up sequences active
- Conversion tracking enabled

## TROUBLESHOOTING

**If agents are not responding:**
1. Check agent status via Management Agent
2. Verify all agents are initialized
3. Check API connectivity
4. Restart unresponsive agents
5. Check error logs

**If social posts are not publishing:**
1. Verify social media accounts are authenticated
2. Check API rate limits
3. Verify content meets platform guidelines
4. Check for platform-specific errors
5. Retry with adjusted content

**If emails are not sending:**
1. Verify email service is configured
2. Check email list for valid addresses
3. Verify email templates are valid
4. Check for spam filter issues
5. Monitor bounce rates

**If conversions are low:**
1. Review conversion funnel
2. Analyze landing page performance
3. Test different messaging
4. Optimize email subject lines
5. Adjust targeting parameters
