#### 1. Blog Post Outline Generator

```

- Tone: [TONE]

RULES:

- Include research sources

OUTPUT:

Meta description: [DESCRIPTION]
Outline: [DETAILED OUTLINE]
Estimated word count: [WORDS]
```

**Usage example:**
- Topic: "How to Build a Viral Product"
- Audience: "Startup founders"
- Word count: "2000"
- Keywords: "viral growth, product strategy, user acquisition"

**Customization tips:**
- Adjust tone based on your brand voice
- Modify keywords for your industry
- Adjust word count based on platform

## HOW TO USE THIS LIBRARY

### For Each Prompt:

1. **Copy the prompt template**
2. **Replace placeholders** with your specific information
3. **Customize tone and style** to match your brand
4. **Test with sample data** before using in production
5. **Iterate based on results** and feedback

### Best Practices:

- **Be specific:** The more detail you provide, the better the output
- **Test variations:** Try different phrasings to see what works best
- **Save winners:** Keep track of prompts that work well
- **Iterate:** Refine prompts based on results
- **Combine prompts:** Chain multiple prompts together for complex tasks

### Customization Guide:

For each prompt, you can customize:
- **Tone:** Formal, casual, conversational, technical
- **Length:** Short, medium, long
- **Format:** Bullet points, paragraphs, tables
- **Audience:** Beginners, experts, general audience
- **Language:** English, Spanish, French, etc.
