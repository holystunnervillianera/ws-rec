# Pricing Market Analysis & Launch Plan

## Current Market Pricing for AI Prompt Packs (2026)

### Market Range Overview
- **Budget tier:** $3.50 - $9.99 (basic prompt collections, subscriptions)
- **Standard tier:** $14 - $39.99 (focused prompt packs, single use)
- **Premium tier:** $47 - $99 (comprehensive kits, multiple workflows)
- **Ultra-premium:** $200+ (specialized, high-value outcomes)

### Specific Market Examples
- **Bundle pricing:** 800+ prompts + reports = $99 (65% off from $280)
- **Individual packs:** $39.99 (custom AI builders)
- **Subscription models:** $3.99-$15/month (recurring access)
- **Etsy prompt packs:** $3.50-$5.60 (low-end, high volume)
- **Specialized kits:** $20-$200+ (depending on depth and customization)

### Key Pricing Principle
**Pricing depends more on SPECIFICITY and OUTCOMES than quantity.**
- A 12-25 prompt pack with clear outcomes > 100+ generic prompts
- Success factor: Attach to specific job/outcome + target user
